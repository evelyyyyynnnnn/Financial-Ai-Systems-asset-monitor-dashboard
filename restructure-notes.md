# Restructure Notes

The updated brief asks for a simpler website organized around three primary sections.

## Main Pages

The first page should become **Financial Market Overview**. It should present a clear professional taxonomy that can grow into deeper linked subpages later. The content structure should cover public markets, private markets, and alternatives.

The second page should become **Assets** with four subpages: **Equity**, **Fixed Income**, **Derivative**, and **Structured Products**. The equity subpage should retain the provided watchlist symbols while focusing the table on useful market-driven indicators and performance fields. The structured products section should start from **MBS** and include tracking coverage for MBB, VMBS, agency, agency MBS pools, non-agency, MBS bond, Totl, Agg, CMBS ETF, AAA, CMBS bonds, and REITs.

The third page should become **Macro/Risk Indicators**. It should organize policy and structural forces, monetary conditions, inflation dynamics, liquidity and financial conditions, economic growth, and risk sentiment into a logical reference structure. The rate-related concepts from the brief should be embedded into a coherent framework rather than copied as a flat list.

## Design Direction

The redesign should keep the interface **as simple as possible**, reducing the more cinematic dashboard feel of the previous version and prioritizing clarity, hierarchy, and future extensibility.
